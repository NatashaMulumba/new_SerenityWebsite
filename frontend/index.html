Our Services

Individual psychotherapy

Our individual psychotherapy services provide a safe and supportive space for clients to explore their thoughts, feelings, and behaviors with a licensed therapist. We offer evidence-based approaches tailored to each client's unique needs, helping them navigate life's challenges and work towards personal growth and healing.

Rate: R850 per 50-minute session

Couple Counselling

Our couple counselling services provide a safe and supportive space for partners to explore their relationship dynamics and communication patterns with a licensed therapist. We offer evidence-based approaches tailored to each couple's unique needs, helping them navigate challenges and strengthen their connection.

Rate: R1,200 per 50-minute session

Family Therapy

Our family therapy services provide a safe and supportive space for families to explore their dynamics and communication patterns with a licensed therapist. We offer evidence-based approaches tailored to each family's unique needs, helping them navigate challenges and strengthen their relationships.

Rate: R1,500 per 50-minute session

What to Expect in your first consultation

Your first consultation is an opportunity for you to get to know your therapist and for them to understand your unique needs and goals. During this session, you can expect to discuss your reasons for seeking therapy, your personal history, and any specific challenges you're facing.

Your therapist will also explain the therapeutic process, answer any questions you may have, and work with you to develop a treatment plan that aligns with your goals.

This initial session is a crucial step in building a trusting therapeutic relationship and setting the foundation for your journey towards healing and growth.
