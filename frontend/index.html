Our Services

Every therapist sets their own rate based on experience and specialty, typically between R1,000 and R1,400 per session. SerenityBot can match you with a therapist and walk you through their specific rate before you book.

Individual psychotherapy

One-on-one sessions with a licensed therapist, just you and them in the room.

Best suited for: working through something on your own — anxiety, grief, life transitions, or simply needing space to think out loud.

50-minute sessions · In-person or online

Couple counselling

Sessions with both partners present, guided by a therapist trained in relationship dynamics.

Best suited for: couples wanting to improve communication, rebuild trust, or work through a specific conflict together.

50-minute sessions · In-person or online

Family therapy

Sessions involving multiple family members, focused on shared dynamics rather than one individual.

Best suited for: families navigating conflict, a major transition, or wanting to strengthen how they communicate as a unit.

50-minute sessions · In-person or online

What to Expect in your first consultation

Your first consultation is an opportunity for you to get to know your therapist and for them to understand your unique needs and goals. During this session, you can expect to discuss your reasons for seeking therapy, your personal history, and any specific challenges you're facing.
Your therapist will also explain the therapeutic process, answer any questions you may have, and work with you to develop a treatment plan that aligns with your goals.
This initial session is a crucial step in building a trusting therapeutic relationship and setting the foundation for your journey towards healing and growth.
